About Velox

With over two decades of rich history in diagnostic software development experience, Velox Imaging is a leader in incorporating intuitiveness, functionality, design, reliability and affordability in its products.

Headquartered in Toronto, Canada, Velox Imaging quickly became the country’s largest installed software suite among diagnostic imaging facilities. With a team of over 100 people world wide and more than 400 installations globally, Velox cloud-based enterprise solution is ideal for radiology and cardiology practices. Velox continues to evolve, grow and raise the standards of diagnostic excellence.
